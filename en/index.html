About This Site

Who we write for and why

Japan employs hundreds of thousands of foreign workers in its manufacturing industry — from Toyota and Honda suppliers to food processing plants and electronics factories. Yet almost all the information available is in Japanese.

Work in Japan Factory Guide fills that gap. We write practical, honest guides in English for foreigners who are currently working — or planning to work — in Japan's factory sector.

Whether you're a technical intern (技能実習), on a Specified Skilled Worker visa, or a permanent resident taking a factory job, this site is for you.
